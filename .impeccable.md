## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are protocol researchers and technical readers evaluating Vibe's market design and risk mechanics. They use the site to understand model assumptions, verify mechanism rigor, and navigate source-backed documentation quickly.

### Brand Personality
Bold, futuristic, and high-conviction. The experience should feel technically serious and forward-looking, with strong confidence signals and minimal fluff.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, atmospheric, and premium with restrained motion, high contrast hierarchy, and glass-like utility surfaces only where they improve readability. New work should match the current Vibe visual language (Agrandir typography, deep indigo backgrounds, luminous accent gradients) while keeping content-first clarity.

### Design Principles
- Keep dense technical content readable first; decoration is secondary.
- Use strong hierarchy and spacing rhythm to guide scanning through complex material.
- Favor purposeful interaction (clear hover/focus states, stable motion, no jittery effects).
- Keep style consistent with existing Vibe components and tokens.
- Build trust through precision: explicit labels, transparent assumptions, and direct source links.

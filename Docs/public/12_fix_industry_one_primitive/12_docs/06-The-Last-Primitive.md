# Section 6: The Last Primitive

The last primitive is **permissionless derivatives for any token**.

This means a market infrastructure where any token can receive a perpetual market without waiting for centralized listing committees, gatekept market makers, or late-stage order-book approval.

Once that exists, the crypto stack becomes complete:

1. programmable issuance
2. permissionless spot trading
3. permissionless derivatives

That third step matters because it introduces the missing half of price discovery. A token can now be bought, sold, hedged, shorted, and matured through market pressure rather than only through narrative momentum.

This is exactly where **Vibe together with Symmio** fits. Symmio provides the underlying derivatives infrastructure. Vibe provides the permissionless market layer and product logic that makes perps available for the long tail of tokens.

Only then does the industry become self-correcting rather than merely self-expanding.

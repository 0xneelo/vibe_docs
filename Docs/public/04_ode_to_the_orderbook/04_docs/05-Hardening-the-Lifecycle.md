# Section 5: Hardening the Lifecycle

Vibe does not just add another market. It changes the character of the lifecycle itself.

When a token becomes shortable, negative information can be expressed economically rather than socially. Critics no longer only post; they can position. This means weak projects are challenged earlier, hype cycles face resistance sooner, and projects with actual fundamentals have a better chance of surviving the noise.

In that sense, Vibe hardens the lifecycle.

The shift is from one-sided attention markets to two-sided price discovery. Long-only launch environments reward narrative inflation. A perp market introduces rejection, skepticism, and hedging. It makes the lifecycle more honest.

This benefits order books too. By the time a market graduates, it has already survived a more serious test. It is not just popular; it is stress-tested.

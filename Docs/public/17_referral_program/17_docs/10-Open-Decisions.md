# Section 10: Open Decisions

The architecture direction is clear, but several policy and implementation decisions remain open.

## 10.1 Referral Policy

- Should referral depth remain one-level, or expand to multi-level?
- Are referee benefits always uniform, or can campaign-specific variants exist?
- Under what conditions can referral attachment be changed after signup?

## 10.2 Economic Policy

- How should private commercial tiers be represented without obscuring public economics?
- Which rewards are transferable versus non-transferable utility?
- What are the final eligibility and weighting rules for TGE-linked points?

## 10.3 Settlement and Security

- What portion of rebate can be computed in-flow vs claim-based?
- Which signer model and custody controls are mandatory at each phase?
- What freeze and rollback scopes are acceptable during incidents?

## 10.4 Rollout and Capacity

- What launch capacity limits should be enforced for listing throughput?
- Which features are mandatory in month-one versus month-three?
- How should partner commitments be sequenced relative to core hardening work?

## 10.5 Governance Requirement

Each open decision should be documented with:
- owner,
- target milestone,
- policy statement,
- and measurable acceptance criteria.

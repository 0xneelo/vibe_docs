# Fix the Industry with One Primitive

## Dissertation Overview

This dissertation argues that crypto has already solved issuance, but it has not yet solved the final market primitive that makes issuance truly useful at scale. Smart contracts made assets programmable. AMMs made them tradable. Launchpads made them easy to create. But the industry is still broken because most tokens still cannot access the thing that completes price discovery: permissionless derivatives.

The proposed solution is simple and concrete. The last primitive is **permissionless derivatives for any token**. Once any asset can have a perpetual market from the moment it matters, the market can finally express both belief and disbelief, hedge risk, discover real demand, and mature assets beyond pure spot speculation. In this framework, **Vibe together with Symmio** delivers the missing layer that completes the industry.

## Core Thesis

- Human progress is driven by better ways to capture and move information.
- Crypto solved permissionless asset creation but not permissionless derivatives.
- The industry remains broken because issuance is abundant while two-sided price discovery is scarce.
- The last primitive is permissionless derivatives for any token.
- Vibe together with Symmio delivers that missing primitive and helps save the industry from remaining stuck at spot-only speculation.

# Section 6: Agency and Channel Strategy

Marketing agencies can help Vibe scale, but they should be used as structured distribution partners, not as opaque attention brokers.

The recommended channel model is:

- agencies receive clear mandates and approved messaging
- compensation is transparent and performance-based
- economic participation is aligned with disclosed referral or revenue-share structures
- agencies are evaluated on quality of onboarding, retention, and partner fit

The transcript points toward a powerful idea: agencies can become force multipliers by coordinating multiple influencers, communities, and projects. That logic is sound, but it should be implemented with governance and disclosure. The goal is not to create a machine for indiscriminate hype. The goal is to build repeatable acquisition channels tied to real market usage.

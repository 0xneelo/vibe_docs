  Go To Market

   Abstract

This paper presents a go-to-market strategy for Vibe. The starting point is that crypto markets compete for attention, but the most durable attention comes from product differentiation rather than manufactured hype. Vibe's differentiation is clear: it can offer perpetual access for markets that other venues either cannot or will not serve.

That product advantage supports a distribution strategy built around phased launch cohorts, partner onboarding, project-led community expansion, and transparent incentive design. Instead of using manipulative scarcity or misleading urgency, the proposed GTM uses controlled rollout to preserve quality, manage support load, and gather reliable feedback while the network expands.

The result is a go-to-market system that is both aggressive and disciplined: growth through utility, referrals through disclosed incentives, partnerships through aligned economics, and expansion through measurable market quality rather than narrative inflation.

# Section 2: Product-Led Distribution

The strongest go-to-market is embedded in the product itself.

If Vibe is the only venue that can responsibly provide perpetual exposure for certain low-cap or newly graduated assets, then distribution begins with utility. Projects have a reason to onboard because the product expands their market lifecycle. Traders have a reason to onboard because the venue offers access they cannot easily get elsewhere.

This is what makes the GTM defensible. Vibe is not trying to market a commodity service. It is distributing a new market primitive.

That means the core GTM message should stay simple:

- projects get earlier perp access
- traders get differentiated markets
- partners get measurable ecosystem value
- mature venues eventually receive pre-qualified markets

The product itself is the first growth engine.

# Section 7: Metrics and Safeguards

A disciplined GTM needs two things: metrics for success and rules against abuse.

## 7.1 Core Metrics

- number of quality project onboardings
- active traders per onboarded project
- retained trading activity after launch phases
- referral conversion quality
- share of volume from repeat users versus one-time campaigns
- market health metrics after onboarding

## 7.2 Operating Safeguards

- ban wash trading and self-dealing promotion
- require disclosure for paid referrals and promotions
- avoid misleading scarcity language
- avoid promises of guaranteed returns or exaggerated outcomes
- prioritize user education during rollout
- monitor channel partners for low-quality acquisition tactics

This is how Vibe can scale without becoming dependent on manipulative growth patterns.

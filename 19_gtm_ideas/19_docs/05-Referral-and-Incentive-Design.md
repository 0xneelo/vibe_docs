# Section 5: Referral and Incentive Design

Referral systems are not inherently problematic. They become harmful when incentives are hidden, misleading, or designed to reward low-quality activity.

For Vibe, referral design should follow a few rules:

- all referral economics should be disclosed clearly
- rewards should be tied to genuine usage, not fake or wash activity
- incentives should not encourage misleading promotion
- partner tiers should be transparent and rules-based
- any points or rewards system should be explained in advance, including reductions over time

This turns referrals from a hype mechanism into a distribution mechanism. Partners, KOLs, and projects can still help onboard users, but the system should reward long-term contribution and honest communication rather than manufactured urgency.

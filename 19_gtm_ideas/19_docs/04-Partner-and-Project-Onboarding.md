# Section 4: Partner and Project Onboarding

Projects are the natural GTM partners because they already have communities, narratives, and treasury incentives. But onboarding must be selective in terms of partner quality, not manipulative in terms of marketing tone.

The recommended approach is to onboard projects that:

- have a real community or user base
- understand the risks of perpetual markets
- can responsibly communicate with their users
- are aligned with transparent market behavior

Each onboarded project becomes a distribution node. It can introduce traders, token holders, and ecosystem partners. But the framing should always be explicit: access to Vibe is about market utility, hedging, discovery, and liquidity improvement, not guaranteed outcomes.

In this model, partners expand the network because the product helps them, not because they are pushed into artificial promotion loops.

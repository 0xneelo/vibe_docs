# Section 1: Introduction

Go-to-market in crypto often collapses into one word: attention.

But attention is not the final objective. It is only useful if it converts into sustainable activity: real trader participation, project adoption, and repeat market usage. The problem with many crypto GTM strategies is that they optimize for visibility without optimizing for quality.

Vibe can take a different path because its product is genuinely differentiated. It does not need to win by shouting louder. It can win by being the first venue that solves a real unmet problem: derivatives access for markets that are too early, too small, or too operationally difficult for traditional order-book venues.

That changes the GTM question from "How do we create hype?" to "How do we responsibly distribute a product with real structural demand?"

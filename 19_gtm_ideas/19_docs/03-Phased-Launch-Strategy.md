# Section 3: Phased Launch Strategy

A phased rollout is useful when it is used for quality control rather than artificial exclusivity.

The recommended sequence is:

1. **Pilot cohort** - existing testers, trusted partners, and a small number of projects validate onboarding and operational readiness.
2. **Beta cohort** - selected ecosystem partners, project teams, and community operators receive access with clear feedback loops.
3. **Partner expansion** - onboarded projects can invite their communities and trusted collaborators.
4. **Public availability** - once support, product performance, and controls are stable, access opens broadly.

This is not scarcity for its own sake. It is controlled rollout for a complex financial product. It protects users, preserves support quality, and ensures that the early network is made of participants who can help improve the product rather than merely consume attention.

# Section 3: The Issuance Revolution

The history of crypto can be read as successive breakthroughs in asset issuance.

- **Bitcoin** proved digital scarcity.
- **Ethereum** made issuance programmable.
- **Uniswap** made trading permissionless.
- **Launchpads** made asset creation frictionless.

This is an extraordinary sequence. It means that almost anything can now become an asset: a project, a community, a claim, a local event, an outcome, even labor or productive capacity.

In other words, crypto has made issuance universal.

But universal issuance without universal validation creates a new disorder. Assets can exist faster than markets can evaluate what they mean.

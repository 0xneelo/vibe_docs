# Section 1: Introduction

Crypto has been running one long experiment: how do we create and trade assets without gatekeepers?

In many ways, the experiment succeeded. Anyone can create a token, launch it, pool it, trade it, and distribute it globally. That is a civilizational breakthrough.

And yet something still feels broken.

The reason is simple: the industry solved issuance faster than it solved the final market layer that makes assets fully legible. We know how to create assets. We do not yet have permissionless derivatives for all of them.

This paper argues that the next leap is not just a better market. It is the missing primitive itself: **permissionless derivatives for any token**, delivered by **Vibe together with Symmio**.

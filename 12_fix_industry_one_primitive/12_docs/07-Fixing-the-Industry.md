# Section 7: Fixing the Industry

What changes if this primitive exists?

First, issuance stops being the end of the story. Launching a token is no longer enough; the token can immediately enter a derivatives market where the market expresses both belief and skepticism.

Second, price discovery becomes two-sided. Instead of relying only on spot enthusiasm, the market can hedge, short, and pressure-test every token that matters.

Third, the lifecycle of assets becomes continuous. A token can move from creation, to spot trading, to permissionless derivatives, and eventually to mature order-book venues.

This is why **Vibe together with Symmio** matters. Symmio supplies the derivatives engine. Vibe supplies the product and market layer that brings permissionless perps to assets that the rest of the industry still does not serve.

That is how the industry gets fixed: not by another isolated app, but by the primitive that gives any token a derivatives market.

# Fix the Industry with One Primitive

## Abstract

This paper argues that the crypto industry is still incomplete despite massive progress in value transfer, asset creation, and trading. Bitcoin made value transfer permissionless. Ethereum made issuance programmable. Uniswap made assets permissionlessly tradable. Launchpads made issuance frictionless. Yet the industry remains broken because it solved creation before solving the final market primitive that gives assets full price discovery: permissionless derivatives.

At the same time, society is entering an era in which information is becoming distorted by abundance. The internet made information widely available; AI makes it infinitely generatable. The next problem is no longer access. It is credibility. Spot markets alone cannot solve that problem. They allow buying, but they do not give every token a native way to be hedged, shorted, stress-tested, and matured.

The missing primitive is therefore not another launchpad or another spot venue. It is **permissionless derivatives for any token**. Once any asset can immediately enter a perpetual market, the industry gains the missing layer of two-sided price discovery. In this framework, **Vibe together with Symmio** provides the infrastructure that can finally complete the chain: from issuance, to trading, to derivatives, to market validation.

# Section 1: Introduction

Two facts can be true at once.

First, order books are extremely good. They are the natural execution layer for assets with continuous interest, dense flow, and many competing market makers. Second, order books cannot serve the full universe of assets being created in crypto. The long tail is too large, too volatile, and too early.

This is the central problem. Crypto can create millions of tokens, but only a tiny fraction ever receive mature perpetual markets. The result is a broken lifecycle: permissionless creation at the front, selective derivatives at the back, and a large unserved gap in the middle.

This paper argues that Vibe exists to fill that gap. It is not anti-order-book. It is pro-order-book realism. It accepts that order books are the destination for mature markets while insisting that someone must solve the bootstrap problem first.

---

## 1.1 The Thesis

Vibe improves order book DEXs by doing what they cannot efficiently do themselves:

1. Bootstrap perpetual markets for low-cap assets
2. Measure genuine trader demand
3. Harden token lifecycles through two-sided price discovery
4. Feed pre-qualified markets into order book venues

In short: **Vibe discovers demand; order books scale efficiency.**

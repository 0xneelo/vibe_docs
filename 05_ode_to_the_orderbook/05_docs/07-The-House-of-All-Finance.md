# Section 7: The House of All Finance

If finance is to become truly open, then listing must become more protocol-defined and less relationship-defined.

The broader significance of Vibe is that it turns market creation into infrastructure. Once the bootstrap layer is automated, order book venues can stop acting primarily as guessers and start acting as absorbers of proven demand.

That has implications far beyond memecoins.

The same assembly-line logic can apply to any new asset category:

- crypto long-tail assets
- tokenized equities
- niche commodities
- local or thematic markets
- eventually any financial object that benefits from early discovery before mature execution

In this world, order books become the visible top layer of a much deeper machine. Beneath them sits a permissionless substrate that discovers, tests, filters, and graduates markets.

That is how a platform moves closer to becoming the house of all finance: not by directly listing everything, but by being connected to a system that can permissionlessly prepare anything.

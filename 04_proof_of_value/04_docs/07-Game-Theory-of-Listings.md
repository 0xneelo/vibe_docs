## 7.2 The Listing Gatekeeping Problem

An issue exists in derivatives markets (TradFi and crypto since the beginning): **listings are gatekept**.

- **Binance**: Perpetual listings decided by a committee
- **Hyperliquid HIP-3**: Must hold 500k HYPE / $20M USD to participate

This creates a **game theory problem**: "The Game Theory of Listings." Listing agents can only list assets based on **perceived value**, not actual value. Therefore it is more important to **signal interest** than to generate actual interest.

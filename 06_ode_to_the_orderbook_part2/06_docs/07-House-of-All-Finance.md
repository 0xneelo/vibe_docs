# Section 7: House of All Finance

If Hyperliquid wants to house all finance, it must connect to a system that can permissionlessly prepare all finance.

Order books are unmatched at scale. But scale is only useful once a market is real. Vibe helps make markets real by bootstrapping the stage at which genuine participation, risk transfer, and shorting first emerge.

That is why the partnership thesis matters:

- Vibe discovers demand
- Hyperliquid scales efficiency
- Vibe hardens the lifecycle
- Hyperliquid institutionalizes the winners

Together they form a continuous system rather than two disconnected worlds.

This does not only apply to memecoins. The same model can extend to any asset class that benefits from early discovery before mature execution.

# Section 3: The End of Vibes-Based Listings

The transcript puts the problem clearly: every permissioned listing venue operates on two axes, actual interest and perceived interest. The tragedy is that it must list based on the second, not the first.

That is the essence of vibes-based listing.

A venue cannot directly observe the future depth of a perp market that does not yet exist, so it substitutes heuristics:

- spot volume
- narrative strength
- community pressure
- auction outcomes
- relationships
- prior exchange listings

These are not useless signals. They are simply inferior to the thing the exchange actually wants to know: will traders trade this perp enough for the market to function?

Vibe ends vibes-based listing by producing that missing information directly. Instead of guessing demand, it lets demand appear in a permissionless environment first.

# Information & Trade Convergence — Context

This folder contains the dissertation on the convergence of Information and Trade, the crisis of verification in the AI era, and the role of permissionless perpetual derivatives as the "Strait Gate" and "Thumbs Down" for the tokenized economy.

## Key Themes

- **Market Cap as Truth** — In an era of infinite synthetic information, the only verification is costly signaling (tokenization, market cap)
- **The Missing NO Button** — Permissionless shorting at launch; leverage for truth
- **Intent-Based Architecture** — Solver-centric OTC (Vibe) as the mechanism
- **Game Theory of Listings** — Control token lifecycle → control information
- **The Strait Gate** — Matthew 7:14; Vibe as filter between chaos and order
- **The Thumbs Down** — Financializing rejection; immune system of the internet
